# JT-081 - Heuristic Sender Signals

## What Changed

JT-081 adds explicit heuristic sender-domain signals for known ATS and recruiter platforms.
The sender domains live in `backend/app/pipeline/filter.py` as separate ATS and recruiter term groups plus a combined default list.
The existing broad candidate query now uses that combined sender-domain list so Gmail metadata can select likely job-search messages before retained body fetching or later LLM classification.

## Why

FR-2.1 requires a cheap heuristic pre-filter before any LLM call.
Sender domains are one of the lowest-cost signals because they are available from normalized metadata and do not require reading or logging private email bodies.
Keeping the signal list in the pipeline filter module also leaves keyword signals and candidate scoring for their own tickets.

## Matching Behavior

Sender matching accepts exact domains and subdomains of configured terms.
For example, `notifications@mail.greenhouse.io` matches `greenhouse.io`.
Suffix impostors do not match.
For example, `alerts@notgreenhouse.io` and `jobs@linkedin.com.evil.test` are rejected.
