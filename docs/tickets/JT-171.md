# JT-171 - Diagnostic Explainability Notes

## What Changed

Added an explainability card to the dashboard's Phase 3.5 diagnostic comparisons section.
The card defines response-rate lift, explains that rankings are computed from the filtered local `applications` and `application_events` population, and warns that segment comparisons are directional rather than causal.

## Why It Changed

JT-171 asks the dashboard to show definitions and caveats without overstating precision.
The diagnostics widgets already render deterministic winners, losers, and correlation summaries, but they did not explain how to interpret those comparisons.
The new copy keeps the dashboard aligned with FR-3.4 diagnostics and FR-3.6 metric integrity by making clear that the numbers come from local deterministic evidence.

## Usage Notes

Open the dashboard and review the `Diagnostic comparisons` section.
The `How to read these diagnostics` card appears after the correlation summary and applies to the currently active dashboard filters.
